Acupuncture for Pain

Acupuncture is a natural and effective approach to treating chronic pain such as back, knee, foot, leg, and neck, chronic headache, shoulder pain and degenerative disease such as arthritis, osteoarthritis, carpal tunnel, sciatica, tennis and golf elbow, and frozen shoulder. Our patients experience remarkable success with this type of TCM treatment. We are here to help heal your body naturally, safely, and effectively.



Acupuncture for Neurological Disorders

Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine are an effective therapy combination at relieving pain associated with neurological conditions since it stimulates the brain to release neurotransmitters and opioids into the body which normalize the nervous system. Some common neurological disorders we treat with acupuncture include: Bell’s Palsy, facial spasm and pain, headaches such as tension, migraines, menstrual, sinus, and stress-related headaches.

Acupuncture for Weight Loss

Non-invasive, natural weight loss therapy

Over a quarter of the U.S. population are obese and well over 50% of diet products don’t work.
So, what about Acupuncture Weight Loss?
Within the practice of TCM, it is believed that excessive weight gains are mainly contributed by an imbalance of the organ systems of the body. Therefore, the attention is focus on the specific areas to affect weight loss such as endocrine system and kidneys to treat water retention and to stimulate nerve and hormonal rebalance. The spleen and thyroid glands to affect sugar and hormonal rebalancing, also the adrenal and the ovary glands are included to treat weight gain due to menopause and premenstrual syndrome(PMS). Needles inserted into strategic trigger points in the body can help increase metabolism, suppress appetite and burn fat. Acupuncture needles stimulate the nerves under the skin which signals the brain and nervous system affecting appetite. The individual would then eat less and lose weight.

How does it work?

Acupuncture can be effective in promoting weight loss through the insertion of needles. When needles are inserted to specific energy pathways(body points) which can stimulate the releasing of endorphins, the body’s natural “feel good” hormones. This can create calming, relaxing effect which can countermand the need to binge-eat that is brought on by increased frustration, stress and anxiety. In this respect, acupuncture can bring calmness to those who are very afflicted and help them lose weight without resorting to synthetic drugs which can have host of negative side-effects.

How does auricular(ear) acupuncture aids weight loss?

The outer ear has a connection to the entire body. The stimulation of different points of the ear with acupuncture needles affects certain areas of the body. The Hunger point, which is one of the five points, is linked to hunger and appetite is being targeted. Other points are also being utilized. Continuous stimulation can be used with NADA. Combining auricular acupuncture with standard acupuncture can improve the weight loss process

How soon can I see results?

The effect and length of treatment varies with individual depending on the level of the negative effect on the organs system of the body, lifestyle, diet, and imbalances. Some people notice the effects of acupuncture quickly after 2-3 sessions, some may take longer. Incorporate with conventional methods such as mild exercise like walking, swimming at least 20 minutes a day along with a low-calorie diet can enhance the process.
