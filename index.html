We offer variety of services within TCM including acupuncture, acupressure, cupping, moxibustion, auricular therapy, electro-acupuncture therapy, electric heat therapy, and dietary recommendations and more. We have an in-house herbal pharmacy, we prescribe and dispense the highest quality of Evergreen Herbs. Evergreen Herbs is tested for its purity, safety and potency and approved by World Health Organization (WHO) and FDA.

Our philosophy is to promote your body’s natural self-healing, not by suppressing the symptoms of the disease. He has earned Master’s degree and degree of Doctor of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (DAOM), degree in Master of Science and Oriental Medicine (MSAOM) while attending South Baylo University in Anaheim, California. He is also Board Certified licensed acupuncturist in both states of Louisiana and California. He also specializes in Zhu’s scalp acupuncture. As a patient of Lotus Acupuncture Clinic, providing the best care in a positive, professional, friendly environment is our top priority. Acupuncture for Pain

Acupuncture is a natural and effective approach to treating chronic pain such as back and neck, chronic headache, shoulder pain and degenerative disease such as arthritis, osteoarthritis, carpal tunnel, sciatica, tennis and golf elbow, and frozen shoulder. Our patients experience remarkable success with this type of TCM treatment. We are here to help heal your body naturally, safely, and effectively.



Acupuncture for Neurological Disorders

Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine are an effective therapy combination at relieving pain associated with neurological conditions since it stimulates the brain to release neurotransmitters and opioids into the body which normalize the nervous system. Some common neurological disorders we treat with acupuncture include: Bell’s Palsy, facial spasm and pain, headaches such as tension, migraines, menstrual, sinus, and stress-related headaches.



Acupuncture for Psychological Health

Many of us are on a default mode when trying hustle and bustle through everyday life which can create a negative impact on our emotional well-being. Acupuncture can effectively enhance and alleviate negative emotional conditions such as anxiety, stress, depression, bi-polar disorder and insomnia which can be associated with emotional disorder.
