Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Q: How should I prepare for my first visit?

    A: We recommend that you have something to eat before treatment. Wear loose, comfortable clothing that allow access above the elbows and knees. Avoid strenuous physical activity a couple of hours before and after treatment. Patients are supposed to relax during treatment. To be considerate of others, we ask that you turn off or silence your phone as it can be a disturbance during “needle nap” time.


  2. Q: What to expect on my first visit?

    A: On your first visit, which includes consultation and acupuncture treatment. During consultation, we will do a thorough patient intake of detailed health history to accurately diagnose and tailor an effective treatment plan based on signs and symptoms. After discussion, patient will start treatment. Initial visit usually takes about an hour.


  3. Q: What does acupuncture feel like? Does it hurt?

    A: When gently inserted the needle may produce a unique sensation which patients describe as heavy, tingly, numbness pressure and traveling feeling which is temporary.
    Acupuncture needles are very fine, hair-like needles that you can barely feel when inserted. It is virtually painless. For some it may feel like a gentle mosquito bite. Very rare patient report feeling any pain after a needle is inserted, if so it can be quickly removed.


  4. Q: Is Chinese herbal medicine (Oriental Medicine) safe?

    A: When prescribed and administered by a professional, licensed acupuncturist. It is very safe effective to treat variety of health conditions and ailments. We prescribe Evergreen Herbs which is purely natural and naturally effective. Evergreen Herbs follows strict safety protocols and assures safety, purity and potency. Evergreen Herbs follows the standards set forth by the World Health Organization(WHO) and the United States FDA.


  5. Q: How long is the treatment plan? How often do I need to come in for treatment?

    A: Depending on the severity, duration and nature of the dis-ease; the number of visit varies from patient to patient. The sooner you seek treatment the less session you will need. Acute conditions may require a few sessions before patient notice improvement. Chronic conditions may take 5-10 sessions to see significant improvement. We do not like to see our patient suffering from any ailment or dis-ease. We want our patients to have better quality of life as soon as possible. Therefore, Dr. Vu is very dedicated to make that possible. Most of our patients report significant improvement after first treatment!


  6. Q: Can a healthy person get acupuncture?

    A: Yes, a healthy person can greatly benefit with acupuncture!


  7. Q: Do you accept insurance?

    A: We currently do not accept insurance. We aim to provide the best service at the most affordable cost in the area. We'll be glad to provide a receipt for your treatment that is covered by your insurance.
